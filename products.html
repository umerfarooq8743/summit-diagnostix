Multi-Specimen Collection Platforms

Our Multi-Specimen Collection Platforms are designed to handle diverse diagnostic needs in a single, unified solution. By combining multiple specimen collection pathways into one ergonomic system, laboratories can dramatically reduce handling time, storage complexity, and the risk of cross-contamination.

These platforms are engineered to be compatible with existing laboratory workflows and standard analytical equipment, making adoption seamless for both clinical and veterinary settings.

Urine Collection Systems

About the Urine Collection System

Urine containers are essential sterile tools used in clinical diagnostics. Present containers contain boric acid to prevent bacterial overgrowth. However, a sample without preservative is also required when there are long transport times or there is a need for re-sampling.

This requires obtaining another sample — a major inconvenience for both patient and clinician. Our multi-compartment specimen collection systems are intended to solve these problems and optimise specimen handling and transport.

Advantages of Our Dual-Purpose Urine Containers:

✓

Prevent resampling and testing delays — both preserved and non-preserved chambers in a single container.

✓

Workflow efficiency & ergonomics during transport — designed for ease of handling.

✓

Availability of sample for future testing — ensures sample is always accessible when needed.

✓

Sample integrity & stabilisation — dual preservation options maintain specimen quality.

✓

Hygiene & spill prevention — sealed, secure design minimises contamination risks.

✓

Adapting to environmental & sustainability shifts — designed with future sustainability goals in mind.
