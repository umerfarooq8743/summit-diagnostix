Quality Standards

Our Quality Commitments

Five foundational pillars that define how we approach quality at every level of our organisation.

01

Product Reliability

Every specimen collection system we produce is rigorously tested to ensure it performs consistently and dependably in real-world clinical and veterinary environments, every single time.

02

Practical Usability

Quality means nothing if a product is difficult to use. Our designs are validated with end-users to ensure they are intuitive, ergonomic and practical in busy laboratory settings.

03

Continuous Design Improvement

We treat every product as a living system — constantly reviewing field performance, incorporating user feedback and refining our designs to stay ahead of evolving diagnostic needs.

04

Manufacturing Quality Standards

All our products are manufactured to meet applicable quality and regulatory standards, with strict controls over materials, processes and traceability throughout the supply chain.

05

User Feedback-Driven Development

The people who use our products in the field are our most valuable quality resource. We actively collect, analyse and act on feedback from laboratories, clinicians and veterinary teams.

06

Sample Integrity First

Our primary quality obligation is to the specimen itself. Every design decision is evaluated against its ability to preserve sample integrity from point of collection to point of analysis.
