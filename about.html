Our Mission

Who We Are

Summit Diagnostix develops practical specimen collection systems designed to improve specimen handling and transport workflows for clinical and veterinary industries.

We believe that improving specimen collection can simplify laboratory processes for further downstream handling. For this purpose our focus is to develop simple, reliable and cost effective manufacturable solutions and workflow innovations that integrate into existing healthcare environments for optimal sample handling.

Our team of clinicians, scientists and engineers work collaboratively to identify real-world specimen handling challenges and translate them into elegant, manufacturable solutions that laboratories can adopt with minimal disruption.
