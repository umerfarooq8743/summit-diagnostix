About Us

Simplifying Laboratory Processes

Summit Diagnostix develops practical specimen collection systems designed to improve specimen handling and transport workflows for clinical and veterinary industries.

We believe that improving specimen collection can simplify laboratory processes for further downstream handling. Our focus is to develop simple, reliable and cost-effective manufacturable solutions and workflow innovations that integrate into existing healthcare environments for optimal sample handling.
